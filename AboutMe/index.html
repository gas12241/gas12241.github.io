About Me

The first video game I ever remember playing was Megaman 8 on the Playstation 1. In 2008, I got Xbox Live Gold and loved playing online against others in games like Call of Duty, Halo, and Fifa. After transitioning to PC in 2013 through League of Legends, I found a love for esports titles and streaming my progression in them. There are a lot of games that I love playing, but the games I have recently played the most are:
  1. VALORANT
  2. Dead by Daylight
  3. League of Legends
